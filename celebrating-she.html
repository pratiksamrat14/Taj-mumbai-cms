CELEBRATING EVERY SHE

We pride ourselves in fostering a culture that holds our women guests in the highest regard, and nurtures our women stakeholders. Reflecting this culture, ‘She Remains The Taj’ is a celebration of the warmth and spirit of every woman. With this initiative, we reaffirm our commitment to encourage and support women as guests, employees, partners and beyond.
